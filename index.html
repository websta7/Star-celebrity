About Alivia

Alivia Mihayo is a talented young actor making waves in the media industry. With charisma, dedication, and passion for performance, Alivia has already captured audiences' attention and is set for a bright future.

According to IMDb, Alivia is a breakout young actress celebrated for her remarkable emotional range and commanding on-screen presence. She has recently wrapped projects across film, TV, and stage, steadily gaining recognition as a promising talent.

Her debut stage role was as Chrissie in The Ballad of Hattie and James, where she alternated with Luna Valentine. She performed both Saturday and Sunday shows over a three-month run. Reports suggest she was paid around £900 per day for these performances, underlining the professional scale of her debut.

She has also gained experience as a voice actor, contributing to radio advertisements and children’s cartoons. Notably, her credits include voicing characters in Peppa Pig ads, as well as original voices for animated shows such as Magic Meadow Adventures and Galaxy Pals. This work has allowed her to showcase her adaptability and reach younger audiences through beloved characters.

Most recently, Alivia appears as Flo in the 2025 feature film Good People, Bad Things, starring Issa Rae. It has been reported that she earned approximately £110,000 for this role, reflecting her rising value in the film industry.

Additionally, she participated in a Christmas TV advert for the musical Mrs Doubtfire, which reportedly paid her around £5,000. See the advert announcement.

We look forward to seeing her next big move!
